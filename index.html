Introduction

These plantain chips are ultra crispy, so tasty and so addictive!

Recipe #1

  • Time: 40min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
plantain 3 (unriped or not tough)
Salt
Suger		 As Desired
Veg.oil 75cl

Step 1: Use salt water instead of sprinkling salt directly on the plantains. This ensures that salt gets into every nook and cranny of the plantain.

Step 2:Slice the plantains using a mandoline slicer or the slicing side of a box grater. This ensures thin even slices that will cook evenly.

Step 3:Also, do not gather the sliced plantains and place into the oil all at once, I have found that doing it that way leads to several plantains sticking together. When that happens, they will turn out soft and not crunchy. So to avoid having soft ships, place your sliced plantains one after the other into the oil. It seems like a lot of work but trust me, it is worth it because by doing so you will get a whole batch of ultra crispy chips!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Potato Tubber 10
Salt As Desired
Veg.oil 75cl

Step 1:The materials one will need in order to make homemade chips are
1. Deep Fat Fryer (Any brand will work)
2. Vegetable Oil (canola oil and peanut oil work also)
3. Red Idaho Potatoes
4. Cutting Knife
5. Mandoline Slicer
6. Wooden Spoon
7. Bowl
8. Paper Towel
9. Salt

Step 2:Before starting the process wash the Red Idaho Potatoes under some water to clean them off. Make sure to clean all potatoes. Cut of Ends and "Eyes" Using a cutting knife, cut the ends of the potatoes off and any eyes. The eyes are the holes in the potatoes. Peeling the potatoes is optional. Caution: Knife is sharp Slicing the Potatoes Using the mandoline slicer, cut the potatoes into thin slices. Caution: Mandolin Slicer is sharp

Step 3:Also, do not gather the sliced plantains and place into the oil all at once, I have found that doing it that way leads to several potato sticking together. When that happens, they will turn out soft and not crunchy. So to avoid having soft ships, place your sliced plantains one after the other into the oil. It seems like a lot of work but trust me, it is worth it because by doing so you will get a whole batch of ultra crispy chips!

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Bread Fruit 1 ball (Matured)
Salt
Suger		 As Desired
Veg.oil 35cl
Turmeric Powder As Desired
Chili Powder As Desired

Step 1: Peel the skin off the breadfruit and cut the breadfruit into quarters. Soak these pieces in water for ten minutes.Have the salt water ready. Add a little turmeric powder and put it aside.

Step 2: Cut the breadfruit into thin slices and deep fry them in oil. When the chips are almost finished, add a spoonful of the salt water.

Step 3:Remove the chips from the oil and add the chili or pepper powder for taste.
